## 🔴 Mars — Curiosity Rover Update

Curiosity continues to traverse the red sands of Mars, navigating rugged terrains shaped by ancient waters. As it photographs these Martian landscapes, Curiosity seeks clues about water's past presence and potential habitats that could have supported microbial life. The rover's exploration unfolds over "sols," Martian days lasting 24.6 hours, slightly stretching Curiosity's time to unravel Mars' mysteries.
